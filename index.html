Billing

Enables generation of invoices for customers based on specific needs, and is integrated with the chart of accounts to enable accurate referencing of charge codes and for reconciliation.

Collection

Provides channels of collection for both structured and unstructured revenue streams through mobile (App and USSD), web, devices (terminals), and agency networks (banks).

Reporting

The platform provides detailed dashboard reports on revenue collection and management activities. Collections are displayed on a real-time dashboard, and data is presented in analytical format to show the status of revenue management for each revenue department.

Receipting

Customer service & issuance

Why Choose Upturn

Our platform will enable the entire organization to manage all revenue activities.

  • Providing a single point of view for all services offered and payments received by the organization. All payments will be automatically reconciled with organization records where available.
  • Providing analytics with insights on trends, patterns and provision of organization services. This includes statistics on users and their requests.

Our Services

Our platform supports an authority to collect revenue using the following methods

Web portal

Customers are able to create unique profiles on a self-service web portal. This portal provides a breakdown of all digitized services can be paid online.

Mobile App & USSD

Customers can use the USSD platform or download the self-services app which contain all digitized services e.g. for a county i.e. business permits, parking, land rates and rents.

Big data visualisation

We avail handheld devices to support collection of revenues e.g. parking, bus parks, markets, ferry etc. They will also be able to issue receipts, licenses and permits on the spot.

Banks And Agents

Our platform is integrated to banks and agents where customers can pay taxes and service fees through their channels e.g. branches, mobile banking, internet banking etc.
